About

I am a Staff Semiconductor Test Engineer with a Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering from FAU Erlangen-Nuernberg and a specialization in microelectronics. My work centers on production test strategy, cost reduction, and production quality for 77/79 GHz automotive radar ICs at Infineon.

My background spans Teradyne UltraFlex and IGXL development, RF and mixed-signal testing, end-to-end test ownership, release support, statistical data review, and cross-functional technical alignment with design, concept engineering, quality, and product management.

I build practical engineering systems that make decisions faster and more robust: from ATE methodology and yield optimization to Python and VBA tooling that standardizes data analysis, improves repeatability, and reduces manual review effort.
